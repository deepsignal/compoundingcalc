The legendary investor Warren Buffet has advised index investing for his children, after he is gone. We hear quite a lot about indexing ,but to-date there is no tool where investors can plug in their contribution schedule into the system, and see what results could they have generated by sticking to an index. This is our attempt at such a tool.

Over here, an investor/long-term holder, can easily view the historical performance of investments, given that he had chosen a certain index to go with in the past. It should also allow small investors with regular employment income, to plug in some contribution schedule.

Some people would point out that historical performance is no way to guarantee future returns. This is ofcourse true. But it is less true with broad based indexes. Broad based indexes reflect the slow creep up of economy, and hence are more predictable than individual securities. We attempt to provide a visualization for long term index holder as to how their capital has grown in the past, and can potentially grow in the future.
